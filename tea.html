Pickwick Green Tea Lemon has a characteristic light color and delightful aroma. This tea blend combines the refreshing taste of green tea with lemon. Pickwick Green tea harmonizes perfectly with a positive energy and healthy lifestyle. Available in box of 20 teabags.

Green tea* (Rainforest Alliance CertifiedTM - ra.org), lemongrass, natural flavouring, lemon peel (2,5%).

Green tea is one of the healthiest teas in the world – it contains antioxidants and their amount is the highest of all tea types. Unsweetened brewed cup contains fewer than 3 calories and small amount of caffeine (compared to the black tea) – approximately 29 milligrams (8-ounce cup). Actual amounts may vary according to the situation, as the brewing time, the size of a cup or number of teabags used affect the data. The addition of lemon in Pickwick Green Tea Lemon also gives a decent amount of vitamin C.

Tea should be brewed by adding boiling water to one teabag. The estimated time is from 2 to 3 minutes. After that, your delicious drink is ready!

What about something colder? California ice tea!

  • 4 tea bags
  • 240ml freshly squeezed lemon juice (5 to 6 lemons)
  • 110–165g caster sugar, to taste
  • 3 lemon slices

1) Steep the 4 tea bags in 1L of boiling water for 10 minutes. Discard the tea bags and allow the tea to cool.

2) Combine the lemon juice, sugar and 1L of cold water in a large pitcher. Add the tea and lemon slices and serve over ice.
